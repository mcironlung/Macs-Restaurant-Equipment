Design

We are proud to offer an in-house commercial kitchen design service to all of our clients looking to build or rebuild their kitchens with us. We use the latest technologies to make sure everything is a perfect it in your building.

AutoQuotes

MAC'S uses AutoQuotes which is the leading cloud based catalog and quotation based application for the food service industry. This helps us select from over 500,000 commercial products from over 500 different manufacturers. Each product can have pictures, videos, spec sheets, O&M manuals, warranty sheets, catalog pages, CAD symbols, and other associated media.

AutoCAD

AutoQuotes in combination with AutoCAD software allows us to combine two very powerful tools together which helps us to streamline the design process. Quoted items and CAD symbols can be placed directly into architectural drawings provided to us, or created by us.
